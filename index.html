The Story

Five boys. One last summer before everything changes.

Somewhere along the sun-bleached docks of Cape Harrow, four teenage boys share their final summer together before the world pulls them apart. Between salt air and small confessions, they learn what it means to hold on — and when to let go.

Harbor Boys is a tender, unhurried portrait of friendship at the edge of adolescence, where every evening bonfire feels like a farewell and every morning tide brings the future a little closer.
